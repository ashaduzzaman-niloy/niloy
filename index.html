Personal Information
Md. Ashaduzzaman Niloy was born in Dhaka, Bangladesh on 22 July 1998. He is the second child of Md. Abu Ishaque Bhuiyan and Nurun Nahar. He had received primary and secondary education from numerous schools for ten years in Dhaka before appearing for Secondary School Certificate (SSC) exam from Uttara High School and College, Uttara, Dhaka in 2014. In that board examination, he passed with a GPA of 5.00 out of 5. Then he started his higher secondary education at Adamjee Cantonment College, Dhaka Cantonment, Dhaka in 2016. After receiving two years of secondary education, he appeared for Higher Secondary School Certificate (HSC) examination and successfully passed with a GPA of 5.00 out of 5. After completing his higher secondary education, he started his Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (BSc. in EEE) at Islamic University of Technology (IUT), Boardbazar, Gazipur in 2017. IUT is a top-ranked international university in Bangladesh that is governed and funded by the Organization of Islamic Cooperation (OIC). IUT offers quality education, scholarships, a comfortable environment, and a joyful experience for admitted students. Md. Ashaduzzaman Niloy received OIC scholarship for achieving top rank in merit position during the admission test. He completed his Bachelor's degree in 2021 with first-class honors having a CGPA of 3.82 out of 4.
Md. Ashaduzzaman Niloy is currently pursuing Master of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (MSc. in EEE) at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) besides working as a Lecturer (EEE) at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Uttara University, Uttara, Dhaka.
